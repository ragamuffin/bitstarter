Have you ever wanted to move to a different part of the world? Do you know if you need a visa to work in certain city? What would be suitable pay to enjoy an equal standard of living in this new place?

 

Welcome to Relocate Me!

 

Here we'll attempt to answer all those questions with the power of the community. Think of this as relocation advice on steroids!

FAQ

What will this site contain?
The site will mainly provide you means to compare the cost of living across various cities of the world. In addition to that, it will help you understand various legalities associated with the whole process of moving from one place to another, especially if this means relocating to a differnt country. Hint: Do you fancy Dubai? How about London?
How will you gather all the data?
Data will be gathered via the site community! It's you, the member, who'll provide details of the cost of living for your current location, and the same will be done by members who live at other places, and thus, we'll have enough data to compare. In addition the that, this website will pick expert expats and provide guidance on various issues related to adjusting in a new place---ranging from opening up a bank account to choosing a school for your kids.
So expats are your primary audience?
Yes, but this will make equal sense to students as well as expats trying to make a decision to return to their home country after living abroad for several years.
